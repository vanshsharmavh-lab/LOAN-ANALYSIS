# Loan Approval Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes historical loan application data to understand the factors associated with loan approval. The goal is to identify patterns in applicant information and provide business insights that can support better lending decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Banks receive a large number of loan applications every day. Approving loans without proper analysis increases financial risk, while rejecting eligible applicants can reduce business growth.

This project analyzes historical loan data to understand which applicant characteristics are associated with loan approval.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze loan approval patterns.
* Measure the overall approval rate.
* Study the relationship between credit history and loan approval.
* Compare approvals across education levels, gender, and property areas.
* Understand applicant income and loan amount distributions.
* Provide business recommendations based on the findings.

---

## Key Insights

* A majority of loan applications were approved.
* Applicants with a good credit history were approved more frequently.
* Income contributes to loan approval but is not the only deciding factor.
* Approval patterns vary across education levels and property areas.
* Most approved loans fall within a moderate loan amount range.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Prioritize faster processing for applicants with strong credit history.
* Review rejected applications to identify common reasons for rejection.
* Monitor approval trends across different property areas.
* Use historical loan data to regularly evaluate lending policies.
